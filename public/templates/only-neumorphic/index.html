THE METHOD

Six Pillars of
Sleep Architecture

Each pillar addresses a distinct failure mode. Your protocol is assembled from the pillars your data reveals.

Circadian Recalibration

Precision light exposure schedules built from your chronotype. We use 10,000 lux panels timed to your cortisol curve.

Neural Downregulation

Binaural audio protocols and vagal nerve stimulation sequences that drop your nervous system from sympathetic to parasympathetic in under 12 minutes.

Supplement Architecture

Pharmacogenomically-informed supplement stacks. Magnesium glycinate, L-theanine, apigenin — dosed to your body weight and metabolism.

HRV Optimization

Continuous heart rate variability tracking identifies your recovery windows. We adjust your protocol weekly based on autonomic readiness.

Environment Engineering

Temperature cascading, acoustic masking, and light elimination. We audit your bedroom and ship the hardware you need.

Behavioral Scripting

Evening wind-down protocols, screen hygiene rules, and meal timing windows. Personalized scripts replace your chaotic pre-sleep routine.

QUESTIONS

Common Questions

How is this different from a sleep coach or meditation app?

+

We're a clinical practice, not a content platform. Your protocol is built from wearable biometric data — real sleep stages, HRV, skin temperature — and adjusted weekly by a licensed clinician. Apps guess. We measure.

What wearable do I need?

+

We support WHOOP, Oura Ring, Apple Watch (Series 7+), and Eight Sleep. If you don't own one, we'll recommend the best fit for your protocol and budget during your assessment.

I travel frequently. Can the protocol adapt?

+

Yes — travel protocols are built into every program. We pre-shift your circadian rhythm before timezone changes and provide a portable light/supplement kit. Most executives on the Clinical plan travel 2-3 weeks per month.

When will I see results?

+

Most clients report subjective improvement within 10 days. Measurable deep sleep gains typically appear by day 21. By day 42, 94% of clients show significant improvement in sleep efficiency, deep sleep duration, and morning HRV.

Are supplements safe with my current medications?

+

Every supplement stack is reviewed against your medication list by a licensed clinician before dispensing. We use pharmacogenomic data when available. If there's a contraindication, we substitute or remove — never guess.

What happens after the 90 days?

+

You keep everything — the hardware, the supplement formulas, and your maintenance playbook. Foundation and Clinical clients can add quarterly check-ins for $200/session. Executive clients receive lifetime quarterly reviews included.
