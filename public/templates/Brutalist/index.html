What we
actually
believe
about type.

  • Type is infrastructure. It outlives every brand refresh and every art director who commissioned it.
  • Trends in type are a tax on the designer who doesn't know history. We refuse to pay it.
  • Industrial aesthetics aren't a reaction to modernism. They're a return to type's mechanical origin.
  • The best typeface for a project is often the most structurally honest one, not the most decorative.
  • Variable fonts aren't a feature. They're the correct format for type in 2024 and beyond.
